The English Premier League

The current visualization will show how the development of the Premier League since its inception.

It shows the team ranking since 1992 as well as the Premier League top scorers.

Lastly it shows the spread of the Premier League teams across the UK, and the development of the number of forieng players in the league.

Rankings

The visualization on the right shows an interactive bar chart of the premier league team rankings per year. Below the top scorers per year are displayed in a table. The use can pick a given year through the dropdown menu. Both the bar chart and the table will then be updated.

Spread of Teams

The visualization on the distribution of premier league teams across the UK will be an interactive map starting from 1992 until 2014. The user can choose to display a particular year by means of a dropdown menu. The user can also click on certain teams. The average amount of foreign players for that team will then be displayed in the linegraph.

Foreign Players

The linegraph below shows the average number of foreign players in the premier league per year. As one can see, the trend is increasing. The user can select additional teams by clicking on the teams on the map. The user can choose a maximum of 2 other teams. Note that if the number of foreign player in a team is 0, this means that the team wasn't participating in the premier league during the given year.
